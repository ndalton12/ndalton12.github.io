Northeastern University

Northeastern University focuses on teaching through practical experiences and co-ops. I chose to come here because of the strong computer science and physics programs. This semester, I'm taking classes in Modern Physics, Differential Equations and Linear Algebra, Fundamentals of Computer Science I, and Literature and Digital Diversity.

High School

The Massachusetts Academy of Math and Science is a free, co-educational, public school of excellence that enrolls approximately 100 academically accelerated 11th and 12th grade students from Massachusetts. Math and science are emphasized within a comprehensive, interactive academic program. Seniors take all their classes at Worcester Polytechnic Institute while completing a Senior Independent Study Project (SISP). I took classes in algorithms, discrete math, multivariable calculus, and more.




While doing my SISP, I've also been learning about the joys of an awesome shell program called cowsay.
